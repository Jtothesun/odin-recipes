Protein Fluff

"Enjoy this fluffy ice cream and curl for DAYS"

Ingredients:

  • 1 Scoop of your favorite protein powder
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Cup Ice
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar substitute
  • 1/2 Tbsp Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 Tbsp Guar gum
  • 1/4 Cup Unsweetened almond milk

Steps:

  1. In a food processor add the ice and the banana.
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add all remaining ingredients.
  4. Process until a thick fluffy consistency forms (this may take a few minutes).
